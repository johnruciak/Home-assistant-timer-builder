## ðŸ› ï¸ How to Use

### 1. Quick Entry (Fastest)
Use this if you already know the technical ID of your device.
*   **Where to find the ID**: In Home Assistant, go to **Developer Tools > States**. Look for the `entity_id` column (e.g., `switch.living_room_fan` or `valve.garden_tap`).
*   **How to setup**: Paste the ID into the "Quick Entry" field in this app.

### 2. Entity Sync (Recommended for Lists)
Import your entire device list to quickly pick what you need.
*   **Using HACS**: For the best experience, we recommend installing the [Entity Exporter Card](https://github.com/scharc/ha-entity-exporter-card). 
*   **What is HACS?**: If you don't have it yet, [HACS (Home Assistant Community Store)](https://hacs.xyz/) is a "plugin store" that lets you add community-made features to your home. It's a must-have for any new user.
*   **Manual Sync**: You can also go to **Developer Tools > States**, highlight the whole table, copy it, and paste it into the "Manual" tab in this app's Entity Sync center.

### 3. Drop Screen (Experimental)
*   **How it works**: Take a screenshot of your Home Assistant dashboard and drop it into this app. Our AI will try to "see" your buttons and guess their IDs.
*   **Note**: This is experimental. High-resolution screenshots work best.
